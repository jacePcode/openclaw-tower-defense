Game Info

Place towers along the path to stop enemies from reaching your base.

Each enemy that reaches the base costs you 1 life.

Controls:

  • Click a tower from the sidebar to select
  • Click an empty tile on the board to place
  • Click on a placed tower to select for upgrade
  • Press ESC to cancel selection
